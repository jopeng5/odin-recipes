Boba Milk Tea

Description

Hailing from Taiwan, boba is a delicious and versatile dessert drink. Keep reading to achieve milky and chewy perfection!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dried, instant boba (aka tapioca pearls)
  • 2 cups of brewed tea (Jasmine green or Assam black teas are classics)
  • 1/2 cup milk of your choosing (soy, oat, and almond milk all work well)
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup (you can make your own by dissolving 1/2 cup sugar in 1/2 cup water)
  • ice

Instructions

  1. Cook the boba according to the package instructions. You may need to move the boba into a bowl with ice, so it remains a good, chewy texture.
  2. Add the cooked boba, ice, tea, and milk into a cup. You may tweak the amounts according to your own preferences.
  3. Add the simple syrup in, and mix everything together well.
  4. Enjoy immediately or within 1-2 hours for the best boba texture!
